**Mission**: Creating open, verifiable methods and reproducible infrastructure that enable large-scale reasoning systems (like GPT and Claude) to engage responsibly with curated, locally governed knowledge domains

### Detailed Folder Guide

#### 0_Admin
Central coordination hub for the entire project
- Timeline and checklist tracking
- Contacts and login credentials
- To-do lists and notes
- File organization documentation

#### 1_Governance
Legal foundation and organizational structure
- **Board**: Meeting minutes, member list, resolutions, COI forms
- **Bylaws**: Drafts and final versions
- **Organizational Meeting**: Founding meeting documentation
- **Policies**: Conflict of Interest, Whistleblower, Record Retention

#### 2_Compliance_and_Filings
Regulatory compliance and legal requirements
- **Incorporation**: Articles of Incorporation (SCC819), registered agent info
- **IRS**: EIN, Form 1023 (501c3 application), 990 filings, determination letter
- **Virginia**: Annual SCC reports, VDACS charitable registration, tax exemption
- **Insurance**: General liability, D&O coverage
- **Licenses_and_Permits**: As needed

#### 3_Operations
Organizational management and operations
- **Financials**: Budgets, bookkeeping, bank statements, receipts, grant tracking
- **HR_and_Volunteers**: Role descriptions, timesheets, volunteer agreements
- **Technology**: Web hosting, domain/email management
- **Internal_Reports**: Quarterly reports, metrics

#### 4_Programs_and_Research
Core research and program work
- **Program_Design**: Mission/vision, logic model, evaluation plans, funding proposals
- **Research_Projects**: Arctic AI and other research initiatives
- **Ethics_and_Data_Governance**: Consent forms, data share agreements, privacy policies
- **Partnerships**: MOUs, collaborator notes

#### 5_Communications_and_Outreach
External engagement and public presence
- **Website**: Hugo static site for resonantknowledgelab.org
- **Fundraising**: Grant applications, donor management, campaigns
- **Press_and_Media**: Press releases, media coverage
- **Reports_and_Publications**: Annual reports, white papers, publications

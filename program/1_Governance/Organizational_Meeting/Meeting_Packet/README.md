# Inaugural Board Meeting Packet

**Meeting Date:** [TBD]
**Purpose:** First official board meeting to establish RKL governance

---

## What Goes in the Meeting Packet

The meeting packet should contain all materials board members need to review **before** the meeting. Send this at least **1 week in advance**.

---

## Required Documents

### 1. Meeting Agenda
**File:** `../Agenda.md`
**Purpose:** Outline of meeting flow and topics

### 2. RKL Mission Packet
**File:** `../../Board/Mission_Statement_Summary.md`
**Purpose:** Full mission, vision, and program framework for board approval
**Action Required:** Board will vote to adopt

### 3. Articles of Incorporation
**File:** `../../../2_Compliance_and_Filings/Incorporation/Articles_of_Incorporation_SCC819.pdf`
**Purpose:** Official Virginia incorporation documents
**Action Required:** Board will ratify

### 4. Draft Bylaws
**File:** `../../Bylaws/Drafts/[filename]`
**Purpose:** Proposed governing rules for the organization
**Action Required:** Board will review and adopt

### 5. Proposed Board Member List
**File:** `../../Board/Board_Member_List.xlsx` (or create a summary document)
**Purpose:** Names, backgrounds, and roles of proposed board members
**Action Required:** Board will elect officers

### 6. Banking Resolution (Draft)
**Purpose:** Authorization to open organizational bank account
**Action Required:** Board will approve and Treasurer/ED will execute

### 7. Initial Budget (Draft)
**File:** `../../../3_Operations/Financials/Budget_2025.xlsx`
**Purpose:** Preliminary financial plan for Year 1
**Action Required:** Board will review and approve

### 8. Conflict of Interest Policy
**File:** `../../Policies/Conflict_of_Interest_Policy.pdf`
**Purpose:** Required policy for 501(c)(3) compliance
**Action Required:** Board will adopt

---

## Assembling the Packet

### Physical Meeting
1. Print all documents
2. Organize in folders or binders with tabs
3. Include table of contents
4. Distribute at meeting (or mail in advance)

### Virtual Meeting
1. Create a shared folder (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
2. Upload all PDFs with clear filenames
3. Send link 1 week before meeting
4. Or: Combine into single PDF with bookmarks

## Board of Directors

### Michael Brady — Founder & Executive Director

Michael Brady is a geographer and researcher focusing on ethical data governance and human–AI collaboration. He founded the Resonant Knowledge Lab to develop open, verifiable frameworks for secure reasoning systems that enable organizations to use frontier AI responsibly and under local control. His public-sector experience in environmental and resilience analysis informs RKL's mission to align advanced reasoning with public-interest governance.
